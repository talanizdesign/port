About

Three acts, one throughline.

Act I — Foundations

Guadalajara, Mexico

It started with a B.S. in Industrial Design and, within a year of graduating, a promotion, leading the design team and coordinating directly with U.S. engineering, reporting straight to the CEO.

Five years of hands-on design leadership before I'd even left my twenties.

Act II — Expansion

Mexico → Portugal

I went looking for a broader lens: an MSc in Industrial Design in Porto, where I also filed a patent for a foot protective device.

Then I turned back toward Mexico, this time to teach, as Design Professor at ITESM, shaping how the next generation approached experience design, creativity, and prototyping.

Act III — Reinvention

Italy → Netherlands

A PhD in Management Engineering from the University of Padova became the hinge point — the moment design stopped being just craft and started being a business lever.

From there: Senior Product Designer, then Product Design Manager, and since 2021, Design Director — building a 10+ person design practice from zero, leading a 4-year enterprise design system for a €12B client, and winning Red Dot: Best of the Best in 2024.

Now based in the Netherlands, I lead that work remotely for the Italy-based studio. In 2025, I also stepped into an Advisory Board role in Spain, guiding an early-stage startup on product strategy and go-to-market.

I'm interested in partnering with organizations that treat design as a strategic advantage. Whether you're scaling a team, evolving a product, or driving operational clarity, I'm open to a conversation.
